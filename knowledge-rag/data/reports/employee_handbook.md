# Acme Inc. Employee Handbook (2026 edition)

This handbook summarises Acme's HR policies for full-time and part-time
employees. It supersedes the 2025 edition; in case of conflict, the
2026 wording governs unless your offer letter says otherwise.

## 1. Paid Time Off (PTO)

Full-time employees in the AMER region accrue PTO at 1.67 days per
pay period, capped at 25 days per calendar year. Carry-over is limited
to 5 days into the next year; any balance above that pays out at the
employee's base rate on December 31.

Part-time employees accrue pro-rata. PTO requests under 5 consecutive
days require only the direct manager's approval; longer absences need
HR concurrence and 14 days' notice.

## 2. Parental Leave (FMLA aligned)

Birth or adoption qualifies for 16 weeks of paid parental leave, taken
in one continuous block within the first 12 months of the event. The
12-week federal FMLA entitlement runs concurrently. Eligibility begins
after 90 days of continuous service.

## 3. Healthcare and HRA

The company offers two medical plans (POL-MED-2026 and POL-MED-PPO).
The Health Reimbursement Arrangement (HRA) refunds up to $2,500 per
employee per plan year for qualifying out-of-pocket expenses. Submit
receipts via the benefits portal within 30 days of the expense.

## 4. Retirement (401k)

Acme matches 100% of the first 4% of base salary contributed to the
401(k) plan, vesting immediately. Auto-enrolment defaults to 6%; new
hires may opt out within their first 30 days.

## 5. Approvers

| Decision                       | Approver               |
|--------------------------------|------------------------|
| Standard PTO < 5 days          | Direct manager         |
| Extended leave (PTO 5+ days)   | Direct manager + HRBP  |
| FMLA / parental leave          | HRBP + Benefits team   |
| 401k changes                   | Employee (self-serve)  |
| HRA reimbursement              | Benefits team          |
